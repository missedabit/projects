Latest: Further strength expected as mills look to output

11 Jan 2011
  • As realised HR coil prices approach US$800/s.ton, US mills are implementing a seventh increase in three months.
  • A temporary disruption to industrial gas supplies to integrated mills in the Detroit area, and heavy snows centred on the Midwest, have tightened supply fundamentals in the short-term.
  • March and increasingly April looks a done deal for the mills, but a pullback in prices remains likely during the latter part of the second quarter as inventory building comes to an end.
